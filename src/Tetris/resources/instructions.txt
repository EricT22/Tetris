INSTRUCTIONS:

Clear lines, just win. Three second delay before game is started/unpaused.

For legal reasons this is not actually Tetris. 


CONTROLS:

Play/Pause - Esc
Reset Game - R

Move Right - Right arrow
Move Left - Left arrow
Soft Drop - Down arrow
Hard Drop - Space bar
Rotate Right - Up arrow / C
Rotate Left - X
Place Piece in HOLD - Left Shift / Right Shift
Take Piece out of HOLD - Left Shift / Right Shift
